Reach for the sky : Sky-King

Immediately recognizable with its large 3, 6 and 9 hour markers, combined with a prominent minutes scale, the Oyster Perpetual Air-King embodies the ties Rolex has forged with the world of aviation since the 1930s. Robust and precise, the watch pays tribute to the daring pioneers of airborne adventure. Its unique face recalls a shared history marked by records and achievements. Through this aeronautical inspiration, the Air-King encourages each of us to release our adventurous spirit and reach for the sky.

7300/1200A Twenty~4 Automatic

Patek Philippe has launched a brand new self-winding mechanical ladies’ watch, designed to set the pace of life in style for the modern, active woman. A new embodiment of feminine elegance, the Twenty~4 Automatic is the first model in the collection to feature a round case. The steel version with diamond-set bezel is available with a blue sunburst or gray sunburst dial with black gradation. The beating heart of the timepiece is the caliber 26-330 S C self-winding movement, which is visible through the sapphire crystal case back, displaying hours, minutes and sweep seconds, and the date in a small aperture at 6 o’clock.

Longines Spirit 37mm : Timeless Precision, Modern Elegance

Step into the world of aviation-inspired excellence with the Longines Spirit 37mm, a watch that marries tradition with innovation. Encased in a perfectly sized 37mm stainless steel case, this timepiece is designed for those who appreciate understated sophistication without compromising on functionality. Featuring a sleek silver dial adorned with bold Arabic numerals and Super-LumiNova®-coated hands, the Spirit guarantees legibility in all conditions. The automatic movement, certified as a chronometer, ensures unparalleled precision—a tribute to Longines' rich history in aiding explorers and pioneers.
